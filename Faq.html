Frequently asked questions

How long will it take for me to hear back from Discount Bee?

We’ll aim to get back to you as soon as we can – once your enquiry has been submitted, we’ll make sure it reaches the relevant department, who will, in turn, reach out to you.

I’m a student, how do I reach your student support team?

If you have a question about using Discount Bee as a student, or if you’re having trouble logging into your account, please do visit our Help Centre: help.studentbeans.com/hc/en-us
